Rules

General Server Rules

  1. No Griefing

    2. Griefing is strictly prohibited and carries a maximum penalty of a permanent ban.

  2. No Advertising

    3. Mentioning the IP address, communication channels and/or the social media accounts of non-partner servers is not allowed. However, you may still mention other servers or casually chat about them.

  3. Do not block trains or trespass on train tracks

    4. Unless given special permission by the railroad company, standing on tracks will result in a fine.

  4. Do not steal or engage in PvP combat

    5. Theft and PvP is stricty prohibited and will result in a fine, and the confiscation of your inventory and ender chest.

  5. Graffiti art and street trash is allowed

    6. In order to spice up our server, we have permitted everyone to build on the side of empty walls, and on streets. However, such art is not allowed inside tunnels or railroad property, unless the owner of said tunnel or railroad says otherwise.

  6. No doxxing

    7. Do not disseminate any private information about any player, not limited to but including the real name, email address and pictures of faces. Limaru values internet privacy and will not hesitate to take legal action if the privacy of individuals are infringed.

  7. No DM protesting

    8. Do not message the lawmakers of Limaru to change a law. Parliament exists for a reason.

  8. Respect human rights

    9. All players are human like you. Do not infringe their rights.

  9. Do not aid in crime

    10. It is an offence to aid someone else in crime.

  10. Do not wage war on Limaru, or as Limaru

    11. Waging war against Limaru, either in a roleplay sense or by attacking the server, or waging war against other servers in Limaru's name will get you punished.

Building-specific Rules

  1. Do not use //fast

    2. As per the Faster WorldEdit Act 2019, the WorldEdit command //fast is not permitted for any rank. This is to ensure proper CoreProtect logging.

  2. Complete the exterior of your builds

    3. To keep our server clean, abandoned and incomplete builds will be removed by the Architects after 1 month of inactivity.

  3. Follow local building laws

    4. Certain parts of the server may have special building laws, e.g. style restrictions, building type restrictions etc. Please follow these local laws.
